# Phase 12.1: Integrate Razorpay payment system for hackathons - Context

## Objective
Integrate Razorpay payment system (demo mode) to allow Organizers to set entry fees for their hackathons, with flexible pricing models (team-wise vs participant-wise).

## Decisions & Assumptions
- **Razorpay Credentials**: The system will use a single global Razorpay account. The `RAZORPAY_KEY_ID` and `RAZORPAY_KEY_SECRET` will be configured in the backend `.env` file. Organizers do not provide their own keys.
- **Participant-wise pricing logic**: The Team Leader pays the total aggregated amount for the entire team upfront during team registration. Teammates do not pay individually. 
  - Formula: `Total Amount = Fee Amount * Team Size` (if participant-wise) or `Total Amount = Fee Amount` (if team-wise).
- **Frontend Technology**: Standard Django Templates are being used for the participant registration flow. We will integrate the standard Razorpay Checkout JS modal into the existing templates.

## Scope & Implementation Details
- **organizer/models.py**: Add `is_paid`, `fee_type` ('team', 'participant'), and `fee_amount` to the `Hackathon` model.
- **organizer/forms.py**: Expose these new fields in the `HackathonForm`.
- **participant/models.py**: Create a `Payment` model to securely track Razorpay Order IDs, Payment IDs, amounts, and statuses. 
- **participant/views.py**: Intercept the team registration flow to render the Razorpay Checkout JS modal if a fee applies. The registration only completes after the payment is successfully verified on the server side using Razorpay's signature verification.
